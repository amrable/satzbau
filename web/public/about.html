A free German sentence analyzer.

What it does

Paste any German sentence and get a structured breakdown:

  • Nouns — with article (der / die / das) and plural form.
  • Verbs — infinitive, form in the sentence, Partizip II, and auxiliary (haben or sein).
  • Breakdown — the syntactic role of each part of the sentence (Subjekt, Prädikat, Akkusativ-/Dativobjekt, adverbials).

How it works

The front end is a small React app served from Cloudflare Pages. It sends your sentence to a tiny backend which forwards it to a large language model through OpenRouter and returns a JSON structure the page renders as three tables.

Privacy

Sentences you submit are sent to the language model provider for analysis. Submitted sentences and the returned analyses may be stored (without IP address or any identifying metadata) to help improve this tool — please don't submit personal or sensitive information. Cloudflare Web Analytics is used for privacy-friendly pageview stats (no cookies, no personal data).

Disclaimer. This tool uses AI to generate grammatical analyses. Results may be incorrect or incomplete. Do not rely on it for exam answers or professional translation — double-check with a dictionary or a human speaker when it matters.
